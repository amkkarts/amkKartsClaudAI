Our Story

DRIVEN BY PASSION

"Delivering Performance — One Kart at a Time."

AMK Industry — Alle Möglichen Kraftfahrzeuge Industry — was founded with a singular mission: to bring world-class go-kart experiences to India. From our roots in automotive engineering, we have grown into India's premier manufacturer of electric and petrol go-karts.

Our FUNZO brand go-karts combine cutting-edge electric vehicle technology with proven motorsport engineering to create karts that thrill riders and deliver exceptional commercial value for track operators.
