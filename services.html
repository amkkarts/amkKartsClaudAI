Got Questions?

FREQUENTLY ASKED

How long does it take to manufacture a kart?+

Standard karts from our catalogue are typically ready in 2–4 weeks from order confirmation. Custom karts with unique colour schemes or special modifications may take 4–6 weeks. We'll provide a precise timeline when you place your order.

Do you deliver karts across India?+

Yes, we deliver to all major cities and states across India. We handle packaging and freight logistics. Delivery timelines and costs vary by location — contact us for a delivery quote to your specific address.

What is the minimum order quantity for a commercial fleet?+

There's no strict minimum — we welcome single-kart orders as well as bulk fleet orders. However, for commercial operations we typically recommend a minimum of 5–8 karts for a viable kart track business. We offer volume discounts for orders of 10 karts or more.

How long does track construction take?+

Track construction timelines depend on the scale and complexity of the project. A small indoor/entertainment track typically takes 4–8 weeks. A full outdoor racing circuit with pit facilities can take 3–6 months. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase.

Do you offer after-sales service and spare parts?+

Yes. All karts come with a 1-year manufacturer warranty. We supply genuine spare parts and offer service contracts for commercial operators. Our technical support team can assist remotely, and we can dispatch service technicians for on-site repairs when needed.

Can I customise the kart colours and graphics?+

Absolutely. All AMK karts can be fully customised in your choice of colour and graphics. Whether you want your business logo, a specific colour scheme, or a complete custom livery, our design team will work with you to create exactly what you need.
